"Students are often worried about their chances of admission in graduate school"

To help Students analyze their chances of getting into a particular school, we have come up with a Machine Learning based Model, which can ease this analysis and provide meaningful insights.

Our Model takes in GRE Score, TOEFL Score, University Rating, SOP, LOR, CGPA, Prior Research Experience as inputs and predicts the chance of admission into a University

The predicted output from our model is good enough to give them a fairly accurate idea about their admission chances in a particular university. Our Model would also help students who are currently preparing or will be preparing for Masters to set their goals and expectations rightly
